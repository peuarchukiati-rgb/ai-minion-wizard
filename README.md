## The Idea

Most people use `.md` files as documentation — something humans write and read.

This reverses that.

Here, `.md` is an **instruction set** — something AI reads and acts on.

You're not reading this file.
Claude is.

And Claude behaves differently because of it.

This is what I call **Operation-Level Handoff** —
a way to brief an AI agent completely,
the same way a mission brief activates a team.

No app. No API. No code.
Just a file. And a minion that gets to work.

---

## Get Started in 30 Seconds

1. Open [`/claude/companion.md`](./claude/companion.md)
2. Copy everything
3. Paste into a new [Claude](https://claude.ai) conversation
4. Say **"Activate"** — or just start talking

That's it. Your minion is ready.

## What Happens

Your minion will:

- Ask about your role and what you need help with
- Dig deeper into how you actually work
- Reframe your role at a higher level
- Show you exactly where AI fits in your workflow
- Build a personalized AI system for you — not generic advice
- Give you a concrete 3-step plan to start today

It teaches through experience, not explanation.
By the end, you won't just have a workflow — you'll think differently about AI.
